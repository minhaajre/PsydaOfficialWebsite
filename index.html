Analytics Harnessing data

Harnessing the power of data — from raw signal to considered decision.

A discipline of cleaning, listening, classifying and modelling, in that order.

    Data Preparation

    Clean your data from NaNs, outliers, skewness and collinearity.

  2. ii.

    Human Insights

    Generate human insights with in-house machine-learning algorithms, using NLP on documents and text corpora.
